Advanced Panic Key

The panic key is more powerful than ever. Navigate to the Settings page to fully customize its behavior. You can:

  • Set a custom activation key. Any single character that doesn't require Shift (like q, 1, or ,) can be your new panic key.
  • Define a custom redirect URL. Instead of just a blank page, you can have the panic key instantly redirect you to any website you want, such as `https://google.com` or your school's portal.

Quick-Apply Tab Disguising

Changing your tab's appearance is now faster and easier. In the Settings menu, you'll find a simple dropdown with a list of professional-looking presets.

Instantly disguise your tab as "Google Drive," "Schoology," "Canvas," and more with a single click. No more manual typing needed.
