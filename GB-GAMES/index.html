How To Control GB GAMES

X is the A button

Z is the B button

ENTER is the START Button

R SHIFT is the Select Button

1 is the L TRIGGER

2 is the R TRIGGER

X + Z + ENTER + R SHIFT (pressed at the same time) is RESET
